who we are

Analogue Moderne is an integrated media design and installation company.  Since 1995, we have provided our customers with seamlessly integrated, audio/video, lighting, and related systems.  Our focus is implementing these systems in residential, commercial, and marine applications.

featured products

Lyngdorf TDAI-2170

We are pleased to offer the TDAI-2170 integrated amplifier. Designed and manufactured in Denmark, the TDAI-2170 series features patented built-in room correction.

The simple fact is that no matter the quality of your audio components, a good part of what your hearing is your room.

Lyngdorf has successfully addressed the difficulties often associated with speaker placement and room acoustics in order to provide pinpoint stereo imaging by way of their proprietary room correction processing.

Analogue Moderne is pleased to offer an in home demonstration in order to provide you with the opportunity to make an informed purchasing decision.
